====The Last Mighty Midget====

During World War II, 130 were built. Now only one remains.

-Our Mission-

The purpose of the Landing Craft Museum has six primary objectives. To manage the operation and maintenance of the former USS LCS(L)(3)-102 as a mobile museum. To Preserve and perpetuate the memory of those officers and men who served so valiantly aboard one of the LCS (L) ships of the United States Navy, especially those officers and men who gave their lives in the service of their country. To acquire, preserve, and present the records, relics, and mementos of the Landing Craft Support Vessels. To inform the public of the World War II accomplishments of the Landing Craft Support ships during operations at Iwo Jima, Okinawa, Philippines, Borneo, China, and the occupation of Japan.
